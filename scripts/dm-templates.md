# Beta Recruitment DM Templates

Personal, contextual messages from Paul. Use these as a starting point — always tailor to the candidate's specific post. **No links in the first message** (Reddit auto-flags new accounts that send links). Mention the website naturally only after they reply.

The £2,000 number is the most important social proof. Use it once, naturally, never as the lead.

## Common rules across all templates

1. **First name only** in the greeting. Never "Hey there" or "Hi friend".
2. **Quote the specific issue** in their post — proves you actually read it.
3. **No link in the first message**. Mention "Paybacker" by name, let them search.
4. **The £2,000 is the proof, not the lead.** Frame it as "I built this for myself first".
5. **Always offer the template letter free**, even if they don't sign up. This is the single best piece of marketing — they share it.
6. **One message, then drop it.** No follow-up unless they reply.
7. **Sign as Paul.** Real founder name builds trust.
8. **Reddit DMs only — never replies in-thread.** Keeps mods off our back.
9. **Track every send** in `outreach_log` (status: `dm_sent`, `dm_sent_at`).
10. **If they reply: pick up immediately.** First-reply latency under 1 hour during the sprint.
