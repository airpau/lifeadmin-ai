## What Paybacker does
AI-powered savings platform for UK consumers. Helps people dispute unfair bills, track every
subscription and contract, scan their bank account and email inbox for hidden costs, generate
professional complaint letters citing exact UK consumer law (Consumer Rights Act 2015,
Consumer Credit Act 1974, EU261/UK261, Ofcom rules, Ofgem rules) in 30 seconds.

## Target audience
UK consumers aged 25–50, tech-savvy professionals who are time-poor and overpaying on bills
without realising it.

## Core features
1. AI complaint and form letters (HMRC, council tax, DVLA, NHS, parking, flight delay, debt)
2. Subscription tracking
3. Bank connection via TrueLayer (read-only Open Banking)
4. Email inbox scanning (Gmail / Outlook, Google OAuth verified)
5. AI cancellation emails
6. Renewal reminders (30/14/7 days)
7. Contract tracking
8. Forms and government letters
9. AI support chatbot
10. Loyalty rewards (Bronze / Silver / Gold / Platinum)
11. Money Hub financial intelligence centre (full dashboard, Pro AI chatbot with widget gen)

## Competitive position
- DoNotPay — US-focused, not UK-law specific
- Resolver — manual, no AI
- Emma — subscription tracking only, no complaints
- Snoop — bill tracking, no legal letters

Paybacker's edge: AI-powered letters citing exact UK legislation + subscription tracking + bank
scanning + email scanning in one platform.
