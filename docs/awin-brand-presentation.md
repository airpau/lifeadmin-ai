# Paybacker — Brand Presentation

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Paybacker
**Tagline:** Your AI-Powered Money Assistant
**Mission:** To give every UK consumer the tools and knowledge to stop overpaying — automatically.

## Visual Identity

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Primary colour | Amber — #f59e0b |
| Background | Deep slate — #0f172a |
| Text | White / slate-300 |
| Accent | Green (savings), Purple (Pro tier) |
| Logo style | Wordmark with amber accent on "backer" |
| Typography | Clean modern sans-serif, bold headlines |

## Brand Voice

- **Direct** — we say what we mean, no jargon
- **Confident** — we know UK consumer law, you should too
- **On your side** — always the consumer's advocate, never corporate
- **Practical** — we tell you exactly what to do and how
- **British** — British spelling, £ symbols, UK-specific references

## Key Messages

1. "Stop overpaying. Start recovering."
2. "AI that knows your rights — and uses them."
3. "The average UK adult wastes £312/year on forgotten subscriptions."
4. "Generate a legally-backed complaint letter in 30 seconds."
5. "Connected to your bank. Working for you 24/7."

## Target Market

**Primary:** UK adults 30–45, urban professionals, dual-income households
**Secondary:** Self-employed and freelancers managing business + personal finances
**Tertiary:** Older homeowners (50–65) with high utility and insurance costs

## Competitive Positioning

| Competitor | Weakness | Paybacker Advantage |
|------------|----------|---------------------|
| Resolver | Manual, no AI, no bank data | Fully automated, AI-powered |
| DoNotPay | US-focused, limited UK law | UK-only, built on UK legislation |
| MoneySavingExpert | Editorial, not personalised | Personalised to your actual bills |
| Comparethemarket | Generic comparison, no action | Takes action, not just comparison |

- **Responsive contact** — hello@paybacker.co.uk for any partner queries
