# Paybacker — Revenue Projection 2026-2027
*Prepared: 22 March 2026*
*For: Paul Airey, Paybacker LTD*

---

## Executive Summary

Paybacker has two revenue streams: subscription fees (Essential £9.99/mo, Pro £19.99/mo) and affiliate commissions from deal switching. With the expanded feature set (bank scanning, government forms, debt response, spending intelligence, loyalty rewards, referral system), we project significantly higher conversion and retention than the original model.

**Conservative projection:** £180K ARR by Month 12 (5,000 users)
**Optimistic projection:** £420K ARR by Month 12 (10,000 users)

### 3. Referral Revenue (Indirect)

Each referral that converts to paid = reduced CAC. With 100-point incentive for referrer + free month for referred:
- Cost of free month: £9.99
- Expected LTV of referred user: £120+ (12 months average)
- ROI per referral: 12x
