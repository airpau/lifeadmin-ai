# UGC Creator Brief — Paybacker

**Purpose:** The single document every UGC creator receives before filming. Attached to every JoinBrands / Collabstr job spec and every direct outreach email.

## About Paybacker

Paybacker is a UK AI tool that helps normal people fight unfair bills. You type in what happened (e.g. "my broadband went up £10 a month mid-contract" or "I got a £100 parking fine from a hospital car park I only stayed at for 15 minutes"), and it writes you a formal complaint letter citing the exact UK consumer law in about 30 seconds. Free to try — 3 letters a month on the free plan, forever.

**Website:** paybacker.co.uk
**UK only.** **FCA-authorised. ICO registered.** Safe, regulated, real.

## Who it's for

Normal working people — 25-50 — who are constantly being ripped off by small amounts (£30 here, £80 there) that aren't worth hiring a solicitor over. The classic scenarios:

- A mid-contract broadband or mobile price rise you didn't notice
- A private parking fine that feels unfair
- An energy bill with a strange "adjustment"
- A gym or streaming subscription you tried to cancel and they kept charging
- A flight that was delayed and the airline fobbed you off
- Council tax that feels too high compared to your neighbours

Each one individually is small. Collectively, UK households lose an estimated £1,000+ a year to this stuff.

## The core message (the one thing viewers should remember)

**It's now as easy for a normal person to fight back as it is for a company to rip them off.**

That's the entire value proposition. Every video should land this feeling.

## Tone

- **Real.** Talking to a mate, not a camera.
- **Slightly angry.** Directed at the company / system, never at the viewer.
- **Specific.** "£42 parking fine at Nuffield Hospital Leeds" beats "a parking fine somewhere."
- **Honest.** If it didn't work, say so. We'd rather hear real stories than polished ones.
- **British.** Speak the way you'd speak to your mum or your flatmate. Not American YouTuber energy.

## Format requirements

- **Length:** 30-60 seconds. Vertical (9:16).
- **Opening:** First 2 seconds must hook. Problem stated out loud in the first sentence.
- **Product shot:** At least one clear shot of the paybacker.co.uk website on your phone or laptop. Screen recording is fine — ideally show yourself typing in the problem and the letter generating.
- **Ending:** Say "paybacker.co.uk — free to try." Show the URL on screen in the last 3 seconds.
- **Captions:** Burned-in captions required (85% of viewers watch on mute).
- **No music over your voice** — music under your voice is fine.
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 minimum.

## What NOT to do

- **No green-screens / filter transformations.** Keep it real.
- **No "link in bio" as the only CTA.** Say the URL out loud.
- **No "this saved me thousands!" unless it actually did and you're willing to be named** — vague over-promising is both unethical and non-compliant (we're FCA-authorised and testimonial claims have to be truthful).
- **No legal advice.** Don't say "this is definitely illegal" or "you'll definitely win." Say "this tool wrote me a letter that cited the law and got my fine cancelled" — describe your experience, not the legal outcome in general.
- **Don't mention competitor products by name** (DoNotPay, Resolver, etc.).
- **Don't say Paybacker is a solicitor / law firm.** It isn't. It's an AI tool that writes letters citing the law.
