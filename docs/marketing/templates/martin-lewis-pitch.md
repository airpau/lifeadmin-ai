# Martin Lewis — Dedicated Pitch Template

**Priority:** Tier 1. A single Martin Lewis mention is worth more than 50 cold pitches. But he gets thousands of emails and ignores most. This needs its own approach.

## Principles

- **Never pitch the product directly.** He doesn't endorse products. He endorses *information* and *consumer rights knowledge*.
- **Lead with something you know that he might not.** One niche UK consumer-law angle where Paybacker has generated real, measurable recoveries.
- **Never use generic "money-saving" framing.** He invented it. He'll smell a copycat instantly.
- **Ask for nothing specific.** Send information as a gift, not a request.

## The email

**Subject:** Niche UK consumer law angle you might find interesting (no ask)

---

Hi Martin,

Genuine long shot. Feel free to ignore — no ask attached.

I run Paybacker (paybacker.co.uk), a tool that drafts formal UK consumer complaint letters using AI. In the last [N weeks], we've had users recover about £[X] across [Y] disputes. One pattern is so clean I thought you might want to see the data.

**[The angle.** One paragraph. Options to rotate through:

- **Private parking (POFA 2012):** "Of private parking fines formally appealed using the POFA 2012 Schedule 4 paragraph 5 procedure, ~87% are cancelled or overturned. Of unappealed fines, ~100% are paid. The drop-off between 'considered appealing' and 'actually drafting the letter' is the entire market."

- **Mid-contract broadband hikes:** "Since the April 2025 Ofcom rules requiring fixed-in-pounds price notification, providers who use vague 'CPI+3.9%' language are technically non-compliant and a formal Section 75 / Ofcom code citation is enough to force a free exit. Most users don't know this and roll over."

- **UK261 flight delays:** "Airlines deny ~60% of first-time UK261 claims even when the flight is eligible. Automated denials drop to near-zero when the claimant cites Articles 5 and 7 directly plus the CJEU Sturgeon ruling. Users who send this template are twice as likely to be paid."

- **Council tax bands:** "The VOA challenge success rate is ~50% when the claimant provides three comparable-band neighbours as evidence. Most people don't know the 'three comparables' rule."

Pick whichever is freshest with hard numbers in our database.]

I'm not pitching Paybacker. Just thought you'd want the data. If it's useful to your team, happy to send the raw numbers — about 4 minutes of your time, no follow-up.

Best,
Paul Airey
Founder, Paybacker
paybacker.co.uk

---

## Why this works

- **No ask.** He's been burned by a decade of founders pitching him. Absence of an ask is a signal.
- **Real data, not opinion.** The numbers do the work. He respects hard data.
- **Specific legislation.** Citing POFA 2012 / UK261 / Section 75 correctly signals you're not a chancer.
- **Easy out for him.** Nothing at stake if he ignores — he's not rejecting anyone.

## If he replies

All actions within 24 hours:

- Send raw data as a one-page PDF. No product pitch attached.
- If he asks about the product, explain in one paragraph — and offer to give his team free Pro accounts so they can test it on reader questions.
- Never mention his name in marketing without explicit written permission.

## If he doesn't reply

Don't follow up. Ever. One email only. If you pester Martin Lewis you join his mental block-list forever.

## Alternatives if direct email fails

- **MSE forum:** Answer real consumer-rights questions in the forum as a helpful expert (Paul, not branded as Paybacker). If you're consistently useful over 6-8 weeks, you may get noticed organically. Never drop product links in the forum — instant ban.
- **ITV team:** Martin's TV team (The Martin Lewis Money Show) takes story pitches via ITV's press desk. Submit a tight story pitch via the normal ITV press route with the same data — they're less saturated than his personal inbox.
- **Guest on a podcast he listens to:** Ask Money Saving Central, Iona Bain's show, or Felicity Hannah's BBC show to host you. Exposure adjacent to his world without directly pitching him.

## Send timing

Not at launch. Wait until you have **at least 50 real case studies with total recovery > £15,000** in the database. Numbers earn the right to the email. Send once the dataset is strong — then never send again.
