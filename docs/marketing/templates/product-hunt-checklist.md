# Product Hunt Launch — Checklist

**Launch day target:** Tuesday or Wednesday. Never Monday (overcrowded), never weekend (low traffic).
**Goal:** Top 5 Product of the Day. Realistic. Achievable with decent prep.

## Pre-launch (T-4 weeks)

- [ ] Create Product Hunt account **at least 4 weeks before** launch. New accounts get down-ranked.
- [ ] Upvote 20-30 products you actually find interesting — become a genuine PH user.
- [ ] Leave 3-5 thoughtful comments on products launched in your category ("Finance", "Consumer tech", "Legal"). Not "congrats nice product" — substantive comments.
- [ ] Follow 30-50 hunters/makers in adjacent spaces.

## Pre-launch (T-2 weeks)

- [ ] Finalise product assets:
  - [ ] Logo (240x240 PNG, transparent)
  - [ ] Gallery (max 12 images, 1270x760 each) — hero shot, feature walkthrough, UK law citations close-up, before/after letter example, founder photo
  - [ ] 1 video (30-60 sec, embedded YouTube/Vimeo — the 60-sec founder video)
- [ ] Write tagline: max 60 chars. Draft: **"AI that drafts UK consumer-rights complaint letters in 30 seconds"**
- [ ] Write description (260 chars): see below
- [ ] Decide hunter: someone with 500+ PH followers. List of UK/fintech hunters to approach 2 weeks out: Chris Messina (global), Fabrizio Rinaldi, Kevin William David.
- [ ] Line up 15-30 real people who will upvote genuinely on launch day (friends, family, beta users, community). PH shadow-bans co-ordinated upvote rings, so real upvotes from real accounts only.

## T-1 week

- [ ] Draft maker's first comment (see `hacker-news-show-hn-draft.md` pattern but PH-adjusted — below).
- [ ] Create a Product Hunt-specific landing page at `paybacker.co.uk/producthunt` with special PH offer (e.g. 6 months Pro for free for first 100 PH signups).
- [ ] Email newsletter (if any) scheduled to go out at launch — "We're live on Product Hunt — would mean a lot if you'd upvote."
- [ ] Social posts pre-drafted for launch day (Twitter, LinkedIn, personal Facebook).

## Launch day (00:01 PST = 08:01 UK)

The PH day starts at 00:01 PST (08:01 UK). First 4 hours are critical.

- [ ] 08:01 — Product goes live via scheduled submission.
- [ ] 08:02 — Post maker's first comment immediately.
- [ ] 08:05 — Send pre-warned upvote list a reminder (private message, NOT a co-ordinated public post):
  > Hey — Paybacker's live on Product Hunt now, [LINK]. If you fancy upvoting, huge help. Grab the 6-month Pro too — yours for upvoting.
- [ ] 08:30 — Post on personal X, LinkedIn, Facebook with the PH link.
- [ ] 09:00-18:00 — Reply to every single comment within 15 minutes. Every one. This is the single biggest factor in ranking.
- [ ] 12:00 UK — Post in the #launches channel of 3-4 Slack/Discord founder communities Paul is already in.
- [ ] 13:00 UK — Email all 15-30 real pre-warned supporters as a second nudge.
- [ ] 15:00 UK — Check ranking. If 5th or better, push harder on outbound. If 10th+, consider cross-posting to Show HN same day for extra traffic.
- [ ] 17:00 UK — Final push on Twitter/LinkedIn.
- [ ] 23:59 PST — Day closes, count ranking.

## The maker's first comment

Posted within 2 minutes of launch, as the maker.

---

Hi PH 👋 I'm Paul, founder of Paybacker.

**Why I built this:**
I got ripped off three times in a year — a mid-contract broadband hike, a dodgy parking fine, and a mis-billed energy account. Each one I could have fought if I'd had the time and knew the exact law to cite. I didn't. Neither does anyone else. Nobody's going to pay a solicitor £200 for a £40 dispute. So UK companies do this on purpose, because they know you won't fight back.

I've got a CS background and spent a decade building systems for law firms like Freshfields and Norton Rose. I knew the technology was finally good enough. So I built Paybacker.

- Drafts formal UK complaint letters in ~30 seconds, citing exact UK consumer law (Consumer Rights Act 2015, Ofgem / Ofcom codes, UK261, POFA 2012, etc.)
- Scans your bank (Yapily Open Banking, FCA-authorised, ICO registered) for forgotten subscriptions
- Scans your inbox (Gmail/Outlook, Google OAuth verified) for overcharges and hidden costs
- Free tier: 3 letters a month forever, no credit card. Paid: £4.99/mo for unlimited.

**Happy to answer absolutely anything** — the maths, the UK consumer-law corpus we trained on, what it can't do, where it's weak. I'd rather be honest here than pitch.

Thanks for the look.

## After launch

- [ ] Write a Day-After maker update comment on the PH thread with final numbers and thanks.
- [ ] Send thank-you email to everyone who upvoted and commented (manually, don't blast).
- [ ] Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to paybacker.co.uk footer.
- [ ] Screenshot the ranking for PR pitches.
- [ ] Reach out to any commenter who seems to have an interesting profile (journalists, fellow UK founders, potential partners).

## What NOT to do

- Don't buy upvotes, use upvote services, or co-ordinate via a public channel. PH catches this; the launch gets shadow-banned.
- Don't post the PH link on subs that ban it (reddit r/UKPersonalFinance, most FB groups).
- Don't email cold contacts with "please upvote me" — only people who know you.
- Don't give up if you're not #1 — #3-#5 is a great result and still drives 2-5k visitors.
