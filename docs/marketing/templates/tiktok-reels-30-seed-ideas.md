# TikTok / Reels — 30 Seed Video Ideas

**Purpose:** The content generation backbone for Paybacker's own social channels (@paybacker.co.uk on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, X). A rolling library of 30 seed ideas Paul or a creator can pick off the shelf any time they have 15 minutes and a phone.

**Owner:** Paul (filming), fal.ai + Cowork (editing assistance).
**Cadence:** 3 videos/week minimum during the 60-day launch sprint, then 2/week sustained.
**Format:** Vertical 9:16, 30-60 seconds. First 2s hook. URL on screen at end.

## Pillar 1 — The Injustice (8 ideas)

1. **"I got fined £100 for overstaying a car park by 6 minutes. Here's why that's illegal."** — Walk through POFA 2012 Schedule 4 para 5: operator must issue Notice to Keeper within 14 days, with prescribed info. ~87% of formally-appealed charges get cancelled.

2. **"Your broadband just went up £9 mid-contract? Ofcom made that illegal last year."** — April 2025 Ofcom fix: price rises must be notified in pounds, not percentages. CPI+3.9% letters are non-compliant.

3. **"Your energy company cannot back-bill you from over a year ago. Most people don't know this."** — Ofgem Standard Licence Condition 21BA: 12-month back-billing rule.

4. **"Flight over 3 hours late? You're owed up to £520. The airline won't tell you."** — UK261 Articles 5 and 7. Only "extraordinary circumstances" defence applies. 60% of claims wrongly rejected on first ask.

5. **"Your council tax band might be wrong. 50% of challenges succeed."** — VOA 3-comparable-neighbours rule. Average refund when successful: £1,000-2,500 backdated.

6. **"Direct debit went up without you agreeing? That's a breach of the Direct Debit Guarantee."** — Guaranteed refund by your bank, no matter who set up the debit. Most people don't know.

7. **"Section 75 of the Consumer Credit Act is the most underused law in the UK."** — Buy anything £100-30,000 on a credit card and the card issuer is jointly liable. Covers holidays, builders, failed deliveries.

8. **"Gym membership you can't cancel? The Consumer Rights Act disagrees."** — Unfair contract terms: onerous lock-ins without clear consent are unenforceable under CRA 2015 s62.

## Production playbook

### Filming

- iPhone, rear camera, 4K, vertical.
- Natural light if possible. Bedroom/lounge is fine — do NOT shoot in front of a plain white wall (looks corporate, underperforms).
- Tripod or ring-light stand. Handheld only if you're walking.
- Record 2-3 takes per video, pick the most natural.

### Editing

- CapCut or Descript for captions. Auto-caption then manually correct every word.
- Short clips (~1-2s) cut together beats one long talking head.
- B-roll: screen-recording of paybacker.co.uk, close-up of a letter, close-up of a phone bill. Cycle 1-2 B-roll shots per video.
- Music: trending-sound on TikTok (muted low) OR lo-fi / cinematic track from Epidemic Sound. Never over your voice.
- Outro: 2-second flash of "paybacker.co.uk — free to try" card, gold on navy.

### Posting

- **TikTok:** post directly from the app (algorithm penalises cross-posted content). Use 3-5 hashtags: `#ukconsumer #costoflivinguk #paybacker` + 1-2 topic-specific (`#broadband #parking #flightdelay`).
- **Instagram Reels:** post directly from the app. Same captions, different hashtag blend (Instagram prefers 8-15).
- **Facebook:** cross-post from Instagram using Meta's native tool — both accounts are already linked.
- **YouTube Shorts:** upload the 1080x1920 MP4 directly, same caption.
- **X/Twitter:** attach MP4 up to 2:20, link in first comment (not in the post — X throttles posts with links).
- **LinkedIn:** only founder-story videos (25-30) repost here. Don't spam consumer complaint content on LinkedIn — it's off-tone.

### Metrics

Log in Supabase (`content_drafts` or `social_posts` depending on existing schema). Track per-video:

- platform
- views at 24h, 7d, 30d
- saves, shares, comments
- paybacker.co.uk visits attributed (UTM: `utm_source=tiktok&utm_campaign=organic&utm_content=[idea_id]`)
- signups attributed

Weekly review: which ideas outperform. Ramp the top 3, kill the bottom 3, generate 3 new.
