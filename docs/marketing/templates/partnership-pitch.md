# Partnership Pitch — UK Consumer-Adjacent Brands

**Purpose:** cold/warm outreach template for distribution partnerships where Paybacker gets placed in front of an existing user base in exchange for value to them. Zero/low cash cost.

## Partnership types we want

1. **Challenger banks / fintechs** (Monzo, Revolut, Starling, Chase UK, Kroo) — "recommend Paybacker when a customer raises a complaint"
2. **Rewards / cashback platforms** (Cheddar, Airtime Rewards, Revolut Rewards, TopCashback) — feature Paybacker as a partner offer
3. **Citizens Advice / charity partnerships** — free Pro accounts for their caseworkers in exchange for referral placement
4. **Price comparison / switching** (USwitch, GoCompare, MoneySuperMarket, Compare the Market) — recommend Paybacker post-switch for contract issues
5. **Insurance brokers / UK estate agents** — bundle Pro access for tenants/buyers with property-related disputes
6. **Existing consumer brands with angry customers** — the kind who know complaints are a cost centre and would benefit from customers self-serving a valid complaint channel (not typically suitable — lean away)

## Principles

- **Lead with mutual value, not charity.** What do they get? Usually: a branded tool their users love, reduced support load for common complaints, affiliate revenue share.
- **Specific asks, not "let's chat."** One deliverable proposal per email.
- **We're boringly regulated.** FCA (via Yapily), ICO, UK-registered company, Data Processing Agreement ready. This matters more than the feature list for legal/compliance teams.
- **First partnership matters most.** Land one tier-1 partnership with a named UK brand; every subsequent pitch references it.

## Partner-type hook paragraphs

**Challenger banks:**
> Your support team probably sees a meaningful volume of "help me dispute this transaction / provider" tickets where the actual fix is outside your control. We'd be an outward referral for that class of ticket. For your customers, it means: faster resolution of external disputes, within your app.

**Cashback / rewards:**
> Your user base is already in "active money-saving" mode — the exact intent state where Paybacker works best. A Paybacker Pro offer sits very naturally alongside cashback: cashback recovers money on what you buy; Paybacker recovers money on what you shouldn't have paid. Your users would realistically use both.

**Citizens Advice / charity:**
> I'd like to donate unlimited Pro access to your consumer-rights caseworkers. The typical CAB caseworker spends ~20 minutes drafting a formal complaint letter per case. Paybacker does it in 30 seconds with the legislation already cited. If that saves even 5 minutes per case, it's a meaningful capacity unlock — and the letters are identical to what your team would produce.

**Price comparison:**
> Post-switch is exactly when complaint questions peak (final bill disputes, exit-fee disputes, cooling-off period issues). Embedding a Paybacker offer in your post-switch email flow probably converts at 2-4x the rate of a standalone cold offer. We'd pay the affiliate revenue straight through the Awin programme (we're already set up as an advertiser).

**Insurance broker:**
> Tenant/landlord disputes, home insurance rejections, and property damage claims all hit a point where a formal consumer-rights letter is useful. Paybacker Pro bundled in your tenant onboarding pack, or recommended in your claims-support flow, would help your customers self-serve the complaints your team can't action.

## If they reply with interest

- **Within 2 hours:** send one-pager PDF covering product, traction (active users, letters generated, recoveries YTD), regulatory stack (FCA/ICO/Yapily), tech integration options.
- **Within 24 hours:** book a 30-min intro call. Paul runs first call alone. No deck; screen-share of the product only.
- **Within 7 days of first call:** send a partnership proposal (revenue share %, go-live date target, marketing assets each side provides, review cadence).
