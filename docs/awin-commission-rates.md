## Revenue Forecasting Model

### Assumptions (per 1,000 active users)
- 30% have switchable energy contracts = 300 potential energy switches
- 5% conversion on energy deals = 15 switches/month
- Average £7 CPA (mix of single/dual) = **£105/month per 1,000 users from energy alone**

- 25% have switchable broadband = 250 potential
- 3% conversion = 7.5 switches/month
- Average £5 CPA = **£37.50/month per 1,000 users from broadband**

- 40% have renewable insurance = 400 potential
- 4% conversion = 16 applications/month
- Average £3.80 CPA = **£60.80/month per 1,000 users from insurance**

### Total estimated affiliate revenue per 1,000 users: ~£200/month

### At scale:
- 5,000 users: ~£1,000/month affiliate revenue
- 10,000 users: ~£2,000/month affiliate revenue
- 50,000 users: ~£10,000/month affiliate revenue

This is in addition to subscription revenue (£9.99-£19.99/user/month).
