# Paybacker — Product Overview for Affiliate Partners

## What is Paybacker?

Paybacker is an AI-powered money assistant built exclusively for UK consumers. It automatically scans bank accounts to find wasted subscriptions, overcharges, and better deals — then takes action using UK consumer law.

## Core Features

### 1. Subscription Tracker
- Connects to UK bank accounts via TrueLayer (FCA regulated, Open Banking)
- Automatically detects every subscription, direct debit, and recurring payment
- Shows total monthly spend, upcoming renewals, and unused services
- One-click cancellation with legally-backed cancellation letters

### 2. AI Complaint & Debt Letters
- Generates formal complaint letters in under 30 seconds
- Every letter cites exact UK legislation (Consumer Rights Act 2015, Ofcom, FCA, Ofgem)
- Covers: overcharges, poor service, faulty goods, debt disputes, refund requests
- 3 letters/month free, unlimited on paid plans

### 3. Personalised Deal Finder
- Compares user's current bills against available deals in real time
- Categories: Energy, Broadband, Home Insurance, Mobile, Credit Cards, Loans
- Affiliate-linked — users click through to switch directly with partner providers
- Personalised: "You pay £45/mo for Sky — switch to CommunityFibre for £25/mo"

### 4. HMRC & Government Letters
- Tax rebate claims, council tax band challenges, DVLA disputes
- NHS complaints, parking charge appeals, flight delay compensation (EU261/UK261)
- Correct legal references auto-included

### 5. Renewal Reminders & Deal Alerts (Essential/Pro)
- Email alerts 30, 14, and 7 days before any subscription renews
- Each alert includes better deals found by AI
- Highly targeted — user is at peak switching intent

## Technical Integration
- Affiliate links use Awin deep-link format with sub-ID for user attribution
- Conversion postbacks tracked via `/api/deals/conversion` endpoint
- Click tracking logged for optimisation and reporting
- Cookie duration: as set by each advertiser programme on Awin
